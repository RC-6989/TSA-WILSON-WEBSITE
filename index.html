    TSA

    A team of students at Donald A. Wilson S.S. dedicated to introducting tamil culture to all through projects and events that enrich the culture of not just our school but our comunity as a whole.

    Our Work

    TSA Movie Night

    A movie night held at our highschool, to showcase tamil cinema to the student body.

    TSA Talent Show Dance

    A coreographed tamil dance at our schools talent show.

    Tamil New Year

    The Tamil Student Association celebrated the Tamil New Year by throwing an incredible party with food, games and music!

    TSA Potluck Sale

    A food sale where members brought different dishes from tamil cuisine to sell to the students.
